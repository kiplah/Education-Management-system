Welcome to Education MIS

This system allows you to manage the educational processes efficiently. Use the navigation bar above to access various features:

  • Register Student: Add new students to the system.
  • Enroll Student: Enroll students into programs or courses.
  • Make Payment: Record payments made by students.
  • View Students: Access a list of all registered students.
  • View Enrollments: See details of student enrollments.
  • View Payments: Review payment history and details.
  • Login: Securely log in to access restricted features.
